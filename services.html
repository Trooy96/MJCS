What we do

From concept to completion — we deliver.

Reliable construction, supply and labour services tailored to Zambia's needs — residential comfort, commercial efficiency, and infrastructure that lasts.

Residential Construction

Custom homes, renovations and estates designed for Zambian lifestyles—modern, secure and energy-efficient.

  • Family homes & gated communities
  • Renovations & extensions
  • Sustainable, solar-ready features

Why us — Local designs that withstand Copperbelt weather.

Commercial Construction

Offices, retail spaces, warehouses and mixed-use developments built for performance and growth.

  • Corporate offices & shops
  • Warehouses & industrial units
  • Fit-outs & turnkey solutions

Why us — On-time delivery with minimal disruption to business.

Infrastructure & Civil Works

Roads, bridges, drainage and public facilities engineered for durability in Zambia's conditions.

  • Road upgrades & paving
  • Site development & foundations
  • Public sector partnerships

Why us — Proven track record in Copperbelt infrastructure.

Materials & Equipment Supply

High-quality products and equipment to keep your projects on track — from vehicle spares to full construction machinery.

  • Vehicle spare parts (trucks, pickups, earthmovers)
  • Heavy equipment (excavators, loaders, graders)
  • Building materials (cement, steel, roofing, fittings)

Why us — Fast nationwide delivery and competitive pricing.

Labour Supply & Workforce Management

Reliable, well-trained labour for mining and construction projects across the Copperbelt and Zambia.

  • General surface labour for mining
  • Safety-inducted personnel with valid certificates
  • Compliant payroll, NAPSA, PAYE & PPE

Why us — Local expertise, fast deployment, full compliance.
