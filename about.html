About MJCS

Our story: building Zambia strong.

Founded in Chililabombwe, Copperbelt Province, Mass Junior Contractors & Suppliers delivers world-class construction that stands the test of time in Zambia's unique environment.

From humble beginnings tackling local residential projects, we've grown into a trusted name for commercial builds, infrastructure and sustainable developments across the region.

With over 5 years of hands-on experience, our team blends local knowledge of Zambian terrain, climate and regulations with international standards for quality and safety.

We're proudly Zambian-owned — committed to creating jobs, sourcing local materials, and building a stronger future for our communities.
