Our Story: Building Zambia Strong

Founded in Chililabombwe, Copperbelt Province, Mass Junior contractors & services started with a simple mission: deliver world-class construction that stands the test of time in Zambia's unique environment. From humble beginnings tackling local residential projects, we've grown into a trusted name for commercial builds, infrastructure, and sustainable developments across the region.

With over 5 years of hands-on experience, our team combines local knowledge of Zambian terrain, climate, and regulations with international standards for quality and safety. We're proudly Zambian-owned, committed to creating jobs, using local materials where possible, and building a stronger future for our communities.
